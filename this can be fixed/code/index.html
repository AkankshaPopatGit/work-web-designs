Product Maintenance and Repair
Made Simple, Affordable and Exciting

Fixing and Repairing Damaged Products

is extremely complicated. It is often easier to replace damaged products with new ones.

But This is Costly for

consumers and leads to more waste and strain on the environment.

What We Do

We work with retailers and manufacturers to make it easier for consumers to maintain and repair products.

How It Works

Vision

A widespread culture of product maintenance and repair that results in significant reduction in environmental waste from consumer products.

Mission

Develop an ecosystem that makes product maintenance and repair easily accessible, simple, affordable as well as engaging and fun.

Benefits for Consumers

Easy access to resources for product maintenance and repair.

Extended product lifespans

Lower costs

Positive impact on environment

Benefits for
Manufacturers & Retailers

Increased brand appreciation,
affection and loyalty

Compliance with Right to Repair regulations

Lower product returns

Lower customer support costs

Brand goodwill with
environmentally conscious consumers
